Visa Services

Explore U.S. visa types handled by my practice, including student, business, work-based, family-sponsored, and return resident pathways.

F-1 Student Visas

Legal counsel for international students applying to U.S. academic institutions, with guidance on SEVIS compliance, OPT, and CPT eligibility.

Investor Visas (E-2, EB-5)

Support for foreign nationals investing in U.S. businesses, including temporary and permanent options tied to capital deployment and job creation.

Employment-Based Visas

Comprehensive guidance on H-1B, L-1, EB-2/EB-3 green cards, and labor certification processes for skilled workers and sponsoring U.S. employers.

Family & Spousal Visas

Assistance with family-based petitions including IR, F-category, K-1 fiancé visas, and adjustment of status for qualifying relatives.

B-1/B-2 Visitor Visas

Representation for short-term visitors seeking entry for tourism, family visits, or business meetings, including support for extensions and waivers.

Returning Resident Visas

Legal services for lawful permanent residents who overstayed abroad and require SB-1 visas to reestablish U.S. residency rights.
