3. What happens if my family member is undocumented? Can I still petition for them?

You can file a petition (Form I-130) for an undocumented relative, but whether they can lawfully obtain a green card depends on their individual circumstances. Attempting this without proper legal analysis can result in triggering inadmissibility bars or removal proceedings.
