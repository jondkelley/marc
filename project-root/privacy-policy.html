Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 9, 2025
Last Updated: February 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, mobile applications, and related services ("Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Forms & Communication: When you fill out forms, request information, or communicate with us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, location, and any additional information you provide.
  • Account Registration (if applicable): If you create an account, we collect login credentials and profile details you choose to share.
  • Customer Support: When you request support, we collect information relevant to resolving your inquiry.
Do not submit sensitive personal information (e.g., Social Security numbers, government ID numbers, financial account information) unless specifically requested in a secure channel.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our Services, including:

  • IP address and approximate location (city, region, country)
  • Browser type, version, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation paths
  • Referring URLs or search queries
  • Date and time of access
  • Interactions with site elements and features

This information is collected through server logs, cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies.

1.3 Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to:

  • Recognize you when you return to our Services
  • Store your preferences
  • Measure site performance and usage
  • Deliver relevant advertisements and content

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact functionality. See www.allaboutcookies.org for more information.

1.4 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from analytics providers, advertising networks, business partners, or public databases to supplement the information we collect.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our Services
  • Respond to inquiries and customer service requests
  • Personalize your experience
  • Conduct analytics and research
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and enhance security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers & Vendors: Entities that provide hosting, analytics, marketing, or operational support.
  • Legal Compliance & Protection: When required by law, to enforce our agreements, or protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Third-party advertising partners may collect non-personal data via cookies and similar technologies for interest-based advertising.

4. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of targeted advertising or sale of personal information.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at the information below.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or comply with legal requirements.

6. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. International Data Transfers

If you access our Services from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Effective Date" above indicates when the policy was last revised. Significant changes will be communicated via the Services or by direct notice where required.
