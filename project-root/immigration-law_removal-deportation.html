Deportation & Removal Defense – Kalamazoo Immigration Lawyer Marc Asch

Protecting Your Right to Stay in the United States

Facing deportation or removal proceedings is one of the most serious challenges an immigrant can encounter. Your legal status, your rights, and your ability to remain in the United States are on the line. At the Law Office of Marc Asch in Kalamazoo, Michigan, we provide skilled, strategic representation to protect you throughout the process.

Whether you are accused of entering or remaining in the U.S. without authorization, or you are a lawful permanent resident facing deportation after a criminal conviction, our firm works to defend your right to stay in the country.

Criminal Charges and Immigration Consequences

If you have been arrested for a misdemeanor or felony and are offered a plea bargain, do not accept it without consulting an immigration lawyer.

Many criminal pleas—even for relatively minor offenses—can lead directly to removal proceedings. The immigration consequences are often more severe than the criminal penalties themselves.

Criminal Convictions

Even minor offenses can trigger removal proceedings and deportation

Immigration Consequences

Immigration penalties often exceed criminal penalties in severity

Never accept a plea bargain without understanding the immigration consequences. Consult with an experienced immigration attorney first.
