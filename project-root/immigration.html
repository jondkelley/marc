Kalamazoo Immigration Law Attorney

The Many Ways We Help Michigan Immigrants

The law office of Marc Asch can help with these immigration law problems:

Family-Based Immigration

If you are already a United States citizen, your spouse, parents and minor children can enter the country quickly and easily. Obtaining a family visa can be initiated in the United States or in your country of origin.

Fiancé(e) Visas

Marc Asch in Kalamazoo can help you and your loved one obtain K-1 status.

Obtaining A Green Card

We work every avenue for obtaining a green card. We also help clients through the renewal process.

Provisional Waivers

Many immigrants are taking advantage of stateside waivers, keeping families together as long as possible.

Adjustment Of Status

We can help you decide if you qualify for adjusted status and assist you through the process.

Naturalization And Citizenship

If you have the opportunity to become an American citizen, which allows you the opportunity to vote, you should immediately retain an immigration attorney for help.

Readiness For Immigration Reform

We suggest that you make plans now for the day that reform arrives.

Removal/Deportation

We will assist you in your attempt to stay in this country. We will advise criminal attorneys of the immigration consequences of your criminal matters.

Orders Of Supervision

Harsh conditions of release following detention can be negotiated. Our lawyer can plead for a plan that gives you greater freedom while assuring U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) that you will return for your hearing.

Immigration Detention

We work with family members to connect with detainees and with detainees to obtain release.

Asylum

We advise clients on eligibility for asylum and assist them in preparing the necessary paperwork. We will advocate for you before the asylum office or the courts.

Temporary Protected Status

We help obtain protection for individuals from a handful of countries in which war is being waged.

Appeals/Federal Court Immigration Litigation

If an immigration judge has denied your application for relief, we may be able to appeal the decision.

Waivers Of Inadmissibility

We help individuals file applications for waivers of grounds of inadmissibility, including criminal-based waivers and waivers involving the three- and 10-year bars.

Preparing For Your Interview

We thoroughly prepare our clients for their interviews, providing tips on what to expect and how to act during family-based or employment-based interviews, asylum interviews and citizenship/naturalization interviews.

Maintaining Status/Travel Documents

We help clients maintain their immigration status and secure the documents necessary to travel overseas.
